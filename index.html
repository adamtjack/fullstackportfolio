Projects

PTSDNET

PTSDNet is an online community dedicated to Veterans talking about living with PTSD and how treatment helped turn their lives around. Here Vets learn about PTSD, explore treatment options and, most importantly, hear stories from other Vets who have faced PTSD and regained control of their lives. Join us today.

Horoscope

A project combining for the Astrological enthusiasts and the curious adventures who want to know more about space. When you login into our site, you can choose your daily sign. Each day, NASA posts a picture of the day, upon choosing your astrological sign, you can then see the daily photo from NASA along with some information about it! We hope you enjoy our fun take on Astrology and Space!

The Grid

Randomly generated map. The viewport of the map is sized based on browser window size. You have health, a level, and a weapon. You can pick up a better weapon. You can pick up health items. All the items and enemies on the map are arranged at random. You can move throughout the map, discovering items. You can move anywhere within the map's boundaries, but you can't move through an enemy until you've beaten it.
